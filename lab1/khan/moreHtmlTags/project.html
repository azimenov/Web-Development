Beshbarmak

  • Time: 2 hours
  • Serves: 4-6
Ingredients Quantity
Lamb or Beef 500g
Flour 500g
Onions 3

Steps:

  1. Boil meat until tender.
  2. Prepare dough and cut into thin strips (lapsha).
  3. Cook lapsha in the meat broth.
  4. Serve meat and lapsha with boiled onions.

Source: Family Recipe

Plov

  • Time: 1.5 hours
  • Serves: 6-8
Ingredients Quantity
Rice 2 cups
Chicken (or lamb) 500g
Carrots 3
Onions 2

Steps:

  • Fry meat until browned.
  • Add chopped onions and carrots, cook until softened.
  • Add rice and water, simmer until rice is cooked.
  • Serve hot, garnished with herbs.

Source: Traditional Uzbek Recipe

Manty

  • Time: 2 hours
  • Serves: 4-6
Ingredients Quantity
Ground beef or lamb 500g
Flour 3 cups
Potatoes 3
Onions 2

Steps:

  1. Mix ground meat with finely chopped onions and seasonings.
  2. Prepare dough, roll into circles, and fill with meat mixture.
  3. Steam or boil manty until cooked.
  4. Serve hot, accompanied by yogurt or sauce.

Source: Central Asian Cuisine
