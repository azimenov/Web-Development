About Niagara Falls

Niagara Falls, situated on the border of the United States and Canada, is a natural wonder that attracts millions of visitors each year. The falls consist of three mesmerizing sections: the iconic Horseshoe Falls, the powerful American Falls, and the delicate Bridal Veil Falls.

Experience the thrill of standing on the observation decks, feeling the mist on your face, and witnessing the raw power of nature. The falls not only offer breathtaking views but also host various activities, from boat tours to hiking trails, making it a perfect destination for adventure seekers and nature enthusiasts alike.

  • World's second-largest waterfall by volume
  • Formed during the last Ice Age, approximately 10,000 years ago
  • Featured in numerous films and documentaries

Must-See Attractions

  • Horseshoe Falls Observation Deck
  • Maid of the Mist Boat Tour
  • Niagara SkyWheel

Don't miss out on these incredible attractions that provide unique perspectives of Niagara Falls. The Horseshoe Falls Observation Deck offers a panoramic view, the Maid of the Mist Boat Tour takes you up close to the falls, and the Niagara SkyWheel provides a bird's-eye view of the entire area.
